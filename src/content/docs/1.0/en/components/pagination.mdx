---
title: Pagination
description: A pagination component is like a digital bookmark, helping users navigate through a sea of content without getting lost. It's a handy tool that breaks down information into bite-sized chunks, making it easier to digest and explore. With a pagination component, users can easily find what they're looking for, without feeling overwhelmed by a barrage of text.
